# Operations — Revival & Injury System (Anti-Powergaming)

## Hard Rules
1. **No instant revives.**
2. **No revives during active firefights.**
3. **No “tap-up and sprint back.”** Injuries persist.
4. **EMS decides survivability** in good faith.
5. **AMU decides recovery** after hospital care.

## Minimum On-Scene RP (guideline)
- **Minor:** 3–5 minutes
- **Moderate:** 5–10 minutes
- **Severe:** 10+ minutes + transport

## Injury Tiers
**Minor:** treat + release / short recovery RP  
**Moderate:** transport recommended, limited function afterward  
**Severe:** transport + surgery likely, restrictions afterward  
**Catastrophic:** may be non-survivable or long recovery

## CPR Policy
CPR is **not guaranteed**. It is a roleplay attempt.
If survival is plausible, CPR may succeed and still require transport.

## Return to Conflict
Players should not immediately return to the same conflict after major medical events.
