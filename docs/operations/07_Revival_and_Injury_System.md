# Operations — Revival & Injury System

## Hard Rules
1) No instant revives.
2) No revives during active firefights.
3) No “tap-up and sprint back.” Injuries persist.
4) EMS decides survivability in good faith.
5) AMU sets recovery restrictions.

## Minimum RP (guidelines)
Minor: 3–5 min
Moderate: 5–10 min
Severe: 10+ min + transport

## CPR
CPR is not guaranteed. It is a roleplay attempt.
