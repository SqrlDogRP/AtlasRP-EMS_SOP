# Operations — Scene Safety & Response

## Staging / Code 4
- EMS **must stage** until Law Enforcement declares **Code 4**.
- If shots are fired, EMS backs out and re-stages.
- EMS may request an escort for uncertain scenes.

## Response Modes
- **Code 1:** Routine (no lights/sirens)
- **Code 2:** Urgent (lights, due regard)
- **Code 3:** Emergency (lights/sirens, due regard)
- **Code 4:** Scene secure / all clear

## “Do not do” list
- Do not chase suspects.
- Do not search people/vehicles.
- Do not move evidence unless medically required.
