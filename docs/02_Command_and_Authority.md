# Part II — Command & Authority

## Chain of Command
**High Command:** Director, Deputy Director  
**Command:** Chief Medical Officer (CMO), Lieutenant  
**Supervisors:** Lead Paramedic, Senior Paramedic, Paramedic  
**Field:** EMT-Intermediate, EMT-Basic  
**Training:** Solo Trainee, EMR

## Authority Rules
1. **Scene Safety:** Law Enforcement controls scene security. EMS does not enter until **Code 4**.
2. **Medical Outcomes:** EMS determines survivability and clinical direction in good faith.
3. **Hospital Outcomes:** AMU determines surgery, holds, and recovery timelines.
4. **Disputes:** On-scene disputes escalate to a Supervisor/Command. Do not argue in public.

## Player Protection
- EMS cannot “punish” players via medicine; medicine is for realism, not retaliation.
- Players cannot force outcomes (“you must revive me right now”).
