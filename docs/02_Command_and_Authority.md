# Part II — Command & Authority

## Chain of Command
High Command: Director, Deputy Director  
Command: Chief Medical Officer (CMO), Lieutenant  
Supervisors: Lead Paramedic, Senior Paramedic, Paramedic  
Field: EMT-Intermediate, EMT-Basic, EMR

## Authority Rules
1) **Scene Security:** Law Enforcement controls scene security. EMS enters on **Code 4** only.
2) **Medical Outcomes:** EMS decides survivability and immediate clinical direction in good faith.
3) **Hospital Outcomes:** AMU decides surgery, holds, and recovery restrictions.
4) **Disputes:** Escalate to a Supervisor/Command. No arguing on radio or in public.

## Abuse Prevention
- EMS may not “punish” players medically.
- Players may not coerce EMS into outcomes.
