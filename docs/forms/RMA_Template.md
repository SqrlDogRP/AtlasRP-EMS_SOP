#### 4. Risks Explained to Patient

“I have explained to the patient that refusing assessment, treatment, or transport may result in, including but not limited to:”[web:58][web:60][web:61]  

- Worsening of current condition.  
- Permanent injury, disability, or death.  
- Delay in diagnosis or treatment of a serious problem.  

Patient was advised to:  
- Seek immediate medical care on their own if symptoms worsen or do not improve.  
- Call 911 / EMS again at any time if they change their mind.[web:65][web:66]  

---

#### 5. Patient / Guardian Statement

“I understand that EMS personnel are not doctors and that their evaluation is not a full medical examination.  
I have had the opportunity to ask questions and those questions were answered to my satisfaction.  
I understand the potential risks of refusing and still choose to refuse the recommended EMS services.”[web:58][web:60][web:71]
