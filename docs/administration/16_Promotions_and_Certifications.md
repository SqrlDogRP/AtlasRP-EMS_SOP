# Administration — Promotions & Certifications

## Promotions (minimums)
EMR → Solo:
- 5 ride-alongs
- FTO sign-off

EMT-B → EMT-I:
- 20 PCRs reviewed
- Supervisor recommendation

EMT-I → Paramedic:
- Critical Care cert
- Scenario evaluation (trauma + medical)

## Certifications
Critical Care, FTO, Life Flight, Fire & Rescue, CRU.
Certs may be revoked for abuse.
