# Administration — Promotions & Certifications

## Promotions (minimums)
**EMR → Solo Trainee**
- 5 ride-alongs
- FTO sign-off

**EMT-B → EMT-I**
- 20 PCRs reviewed
- Supervisor recommendation

**EMT-I → Paramedic**
- Critical Care cert
- Scenario evaluation

## Certifications (examples)
Critical Care, FTO, Life Flight, Fire & Rescue, CRU, Water/Mountain Rescue.

Certs can be revoked for abuse or safety violations.
