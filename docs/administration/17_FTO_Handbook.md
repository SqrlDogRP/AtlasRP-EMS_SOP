# Administration — FTO Handbook (Training That Actually Works)

## Purpose
FTO exists to prevent “paper promotions” and protect scene quality.

## FTO responsibilities
- Evaluate radio discipline
- Evaluate scene safety
- Evaluate medical realism
- Evaluate documentation quality

## Training phases
1) Observe (ride-along)
2) Assist (hands-on under supervision)
3) Lead (trainee runs scene with FTO oversight)

## Pass/Fail rule
If a trainee repeatedly creates unsafe scenes or powergames medicine, training pauses until retraining is complete.
