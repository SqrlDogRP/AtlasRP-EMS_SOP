# Citizen Medical Rights & Expectations (Public-Facing)

This document exists to reduce on-scene arguments. It explains what civilians and criminals can expect from EMS.

## You have the right to
- Request medical assessment when safe
- Refuse care (RMA) if you are competent and informed
- Receive the same baseline care regardless of affiliation
- File a complaint through Internal Affairs (IA)

## You do NOT have the right to
- Demand an instant revive
- Force EMS to enter an unsafe scene
- Force a specific medical outcome
- Use EMS policy to grief or stall a scene

## Safety rule
EMS stages until Law Enforcement declares **Code 4**. If shots resume, EMS backs out.

## “Balanced realism” rule
If you were shot, stabbed, or in a major crash, you are not “fine” 30 seconds later.
Expect transport and recovery RP when injuries justify it.
