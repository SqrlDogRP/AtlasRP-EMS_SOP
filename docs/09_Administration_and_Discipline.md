# Part IX — Administration & Discipline

## PCR Policy
Required for all patient contacts.
Submission within 24 hours.

## Refusal of Medical Aid
Patient must be competent.
Risks explained clearly.

## Internal Affairs
Investigates misconduct.
May suspend certifications.

## Strike System
5 Points – Minor Violation  
15 Points – Suspension  
30 Points – Termination  

Discipline exists to protect realism — not to punish creativity.
